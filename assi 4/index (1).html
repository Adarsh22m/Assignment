Nirmala Sitharaman

(born 18 August 1959)

Nirmala Sitharaman is an Indian economist, politician and a senior leader of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) serving as the Minister of Finance and Minister of Corporate Affairs of the Government of India since 2019.
Nirmala Sitharaman was born in a Tamil Iyengar family[8] in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, to Savitri and Narayanan Sitharaman. She had her schooling at Sacred Heart Convent Anglo-Indian School,[9] Villupuram, till primary level and thereafter at Vidyodaya School in Chennai.
